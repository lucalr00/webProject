Our annual investments

Years Goals

We are proud to announce that in 2023, we invested 185 million dollars in various environmental projects that aimed to protect the planet and improve the lives of people. We supported initiatives such as reducing methane emissions from dairy cows, providing financing and technical assistance to developing countries that want to switch to renewable energy sources, sponsoring some of the greenest festivals of 2023, and funding innovative solutions to tackle ocean pollution. These projects not only helped mitigate climate change and enhance food security, but also raised awareness and inspired action among different stakeholders. We look forward to continuing our efforts to build a better world.

In 2022, we invested 160 million dollars in various environmental projects that aimed to protect the planet and improve the lives of people. We supported initiatives such as promoting climate-smart agriculture, joining a global partnership to help developing countries transition to renewable energy sources, sponsoring some of the most sustainable festivals of 2022, and funding innovative solutions to combat ocean pollution. These projects not only helped reduce greenhouse gas emissions and enhance food security, but also raised awareness and inspired action among different stakeholders. We are proud of our achievements and we look forward to continuing our efforts to build a better world.

In 2021, we invested 320 million dollars in various environmental projects that aimed to protect the planet and improve the lives of people. We supported initiatives such as restoring coral reefs, developing green hydrogen, creating circular economy models, and planting urban forests. These projects not only helped conserve natural resources and reduce waste, but also created new opportunities for innovation and collaboration among different sectors. For example, we partnered with the Coral Vita project to grow resilient corals and transplant them to degraded reefs around the world. We also joined the Green Hydrogen Catapult to scale up the production and use of green hydrogen, a clean and versatile energy carrier. We helped businesses and communities adopt circular economy principles to design out waste and pollution, keep products and materials in use, and regenerate natural systems. And we funded the Urban Forests project to plant millions of trees in cities across the globe, enhancing air quality, biodiversity, and well-being. We are proud of our achievements and we look forward to continuing our efforts to build a better world.
