Green Future

Introduction

Where

Wherever it is possible to build clean energy plants to reduce CO2

When

The project begins with the location of a promising site for the development of sustainable energy (waterways, geothermal areas, windy areas)

Objectives

Our environment come first

One of the objectives of project Green Future is to create structures that generate clean energy, all while minimizing the production of CO2 for the construction of the plants, through the use of clean energy already existing in the area to power the machinery, all use of ecological building materials

A green job

As far as possible, the aim of ZeroCarbon is to employ in the power plants of Green Future a workforce that lives near the power plants, in order not only to reduce emissions (for example by providing public transport systems for workers and families), but also to provide safe work that helps the environment and citizens

Methods

Green education for a green future

Our company also organizes specialized courses to train future employees on the importance of renewables and on the technical aspects dedicated to this sector. The courses are also open to all those people interested in the topic for an in-depth civic education on clean energy.

Funding for research in the nuclear fusion sector

ZeroCarbon also uses the proceeds from the sale of energy to finance research into the development of other sustainable energy: in particular it finances the development of nuclear fusion energy, to guarantee an even greener future
